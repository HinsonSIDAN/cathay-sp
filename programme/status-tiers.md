> **Source:** [Unlock member privileges with Status Points (cathaypacific.com)](https://www.cathaypacific.com/cx/en_US/membership/status-points.html)

Status Points (SP) determine membership status in the Cathay programme (relaunched 2023,
formerly Marco Polo Club). Asia Miles are the separate spendable currency.

## Thresholds (current programme)

| Status | SP to upgrade/renew (within a membership year) | oneworld equivalent |
|---|---|---|
| Green | — (joining tier, lifetime) | — |
| Silver | 300 | Ruby |
| Gold | 600 | Sapphire |
| Diamond | 1,200 | Emerald |

## Qualification rules

- SP accrue within a **membership year**; in addition to the points, the member must fly
  **at least one eligible sector on Cathay Pacific**.
- Upgrades happen as soon as the threshold is reached; only one status level can be
  progressed at a time.
- Beyond flying, up to 100 SP/year can come from the Standard Chartered Cathay Mastercard
  (HKD500,000 eligible spend; HK market only).

## Transition to the 2027 programme

2026 is a transition year: any renewal, upgrade or downgrade during 2026 resets SP to zero
and that membership ends 31 Dec 2026. Status for 2027 is determined by SP earned during
calendar 2026 against the new thresholds — full details in [[programme/changes-2027]].
