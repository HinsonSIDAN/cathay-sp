Cathay's "Smoother, Simpler, Better" programme takes effect **1 January 2027**, with 2026
as the transition year.

## Thresholds from 2027

| Status | SP required (calendar year) |
|---|---|
| Green | 0 (joining tier) |
| Silver | 300 |
| Gold | 600 |
| Diamond | 1,200 |
| **Diamond Exec** (new) | 2,400 |
| Diamond Plus | Invitation only — based on annual fare spending on CX flights |

## Key changes vs the current programme

1. **No SP reset on upgrade.** Multiple status upgrades possible within one calendar year
   (e.g. 800 SP in 2027 → Silver at 300, then Gold at 600; that Gold is valid until
   31 January 2029).
2. **Standardised calendar-year cycle.** SP earned 1 Jan–31 Dec determine the following
   year's status; a higher status unlocked mid-year starts the next day and lasts until
   the end of the following year.
3. **SP rollover (Gold and above).** Excess SP roll over, up to 50% of the requirement of
   the highest status achieved that year: Gold up to 300, Diamond up to 600, Diamond Exec
   up to 1,200.
4. **New tier: Cathay Diamond Exec** (2,400 SP), between Diamond and the invitation-only
   Diamond Plus — dedicated Relationship Managers, elevated service.
5. **Diamond membership reserve.** 5+ years of Diamond (non-consecutive counts) → one
   complimentary Diamond membership (up to one year) per 6,000 SP earned since 2016.
   No expiry; non-transferable.
6. **Mid-status benefits discontinued** from 1 Jan 2027 (those earned in 2026 stay valid
   12 months from issue). Upgrade Passes and complimentary Gold companion membership
   become status benefits instead.

## 2026 transition

- Existing memberships keep their status/benefits until their current expiry (no extension).
- A renewal/upgrade/downgrade during 2026 yields a status valid until 31 Dec 2026.
- **2027 status = SP earned 1 Jan–31 Dec 2026** against the new thresholds; Cathay provides
  a personalised "2027 Status Tracker" during 2026.

All dates in Hong Kong Time.
