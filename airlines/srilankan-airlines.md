SriLankan Airlines is a oneworld member: flights marketed by UL earn **Asia Miles** (rate % × actual miles flown) **and Status Points** (zone lookup by SP tier) when credited to Cathay. Zones and the SP tier matrix are defined in [[earning/partner-model]].

## Eligibility

Asia Miles can be earned only in eligible fare classes on flights marketed and operated by SriLankan Airlines, as well as on codeshare flights operated by all one world® carriers.
