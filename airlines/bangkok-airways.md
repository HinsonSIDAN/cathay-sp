> **Source:** [Bangkok Airways partner page (cathaypacific.com)](https://www.cathaypacific.com/cx/en_HK/our-partners/bangkok-airways.html) and the official Cathay Status Points & Asia Miles calculator (API-observed, 2026-07-14).

Bangkok Airways is a Cathay airline partner but **not** a oneworld member: flights marketed by PG earn **Asia Miles only** (rate % × actual miles flown) and **zero Status Points** when credited to Cathay (see [[earning/partner-model]]).

## Earning by booking class

| Cabin | Fare classes | Scope | Asia Miles | Status Points |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Business | C, D | all | 125% | 0 |
| Economy | H, K, L, M, N, T, Y | all | 100% | 0 |
| Economy | B, G, Q, V | all | 50% | 0 |

## Eligibility

Asia Miles can be earned only in eligible fare classes on flights marketed and operated by Bangkok Airways.
