> **Source:** [Lufthansa partner page (cathaypacific.com)](https://www.cathaypacific.com/cx/en_HK/our-partners/lufthansa.html) and the official Cathay Status Points & Asia Miles calculator (API-observed, 2026-07-14).

Lufthansa is a Cathay airline partner but **not** a oneworld member: flights marketed by LH earn **Asia Miles only** (rate % × actual miles flown) and **zero Status Points** when credited to Cathay (see [[earning/partner-model]]).

## Eligibility

Asia Miles can be earned only in eligible fare classes on below routes: - Between Frankfurt and Berlin, Brussels, Budapest, Dresden, Hamburg, Hannover, Munich, Nuremberg, Oslo, Stuttgart on flights marketed and operated by Lufthansa. - Between Hong Kong and Frankfurt, Munichon flights marketed and operated by Lufthansa. - Between Vienna and Düsseldorf, Frankfurt, Zurich on codeshare flights marketed by Lufthansa and operated by Austrian Airlines. - Between Hong Kong and Auckland, Cairns, Frankfurt, Melbourne, Sydney on codeshare flights marketed by Lufthansa and operated by Cathay Pacific. - Between Zurich and Berlin, Brussels, Florence, Geneva, Hamburg, Stuttgart, Venice on codeshare flights marketed by Lufthansa and operated by SWISS.
