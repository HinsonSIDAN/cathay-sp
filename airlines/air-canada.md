> **Source:** [Air Canada partner page (cathaypacific.com)](https://www.cathaypacific.com/cx/en_HK/our-partners/air-canada.html) and the official Cathay Status Points & Asia Miles calculator (API-observed, 2026-07-14).

Air Canada is a Cathay airline partner but **not** a oneworld member: flights marketed by AC earn **Asia Miles only** (rate % × actual miles flown) and **zero Status Points** when credited to Cathay (see [[earning/partner-model]]).

## Earning by booking class

| Cabin | Fare classes | Scope | Asia Miles | Status Points |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Business | C, D, J, P, Z | all | 125% | 0 |
| Premium Economy | A, E, O | all | 110% | 0 |
| Economy | B, H, M, Q, U, V, W, Y | all | 100% | 0 |
| Economy | G, K, L, S, T | all | 25% | 0 |

## Eligibility

- Asia Miles can be earned in eligible fare classes on flights marketed and operated by Air Canada on the routes below: - Flights between Vancouver and Calgary, Edmonton, Kelowna, Montreal, Toronto, Victoria, Winnipeg - Flights between Toronto and Halifax, Montreal, Ottawa, Quebec, Regina, Saskatoon, St John's, Winnipeg - Asia Miles can also be earned in eligible fare classes on codeshare flights marketed by Air Canada and operated by Cathay Pacific on the routes below: - Flights between Hong Kong and Bangkok, Cebu, Chiang Mai, Hanoi, Ho Chi Minh City, Kuala Lumpur, Manila, Phuket - Economy Basic fare is ineligible for Asia Miles accrual.
