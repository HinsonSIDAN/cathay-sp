> **Source:** [Shenzhen Airlines partner page (cathaypacific.com)](https://www.cathaypacific.com/cx/en_HK/our-partners/shenzhen-airlines.html) and the official Cathay Status Points & Asia Miles calculator (API-observed, 2026-07-14).

Shenzhen Airlines is a Cathay airline partner but **not** a oneworld member: flights marketed by ZH earn **Asia Miles only** (rate % × actual miles flown) and **zero Status Points** when credited to Cathay (see [[earning/partner-model]]).

## Earning by booking class

| Cabin | Fare classes | Scope | Asia Miles | Status Points |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Business | C, J | all | 150% | 0 |
| Business | D, R, Z | all | 125% | 0 |
| Premium Economy | G | all | 110% | 0 |
| Premium Economy | E | all | 100% | 0 |
| Economy | B, H, M, U, Y | all | 100% | 0 |
| Economy | Q, S, T, V, W | all | 50% | 0 |
| Economy | A, K, L, P | all | 25% | 0 |

## Eligibility

Asia Miles can be earned only in eligible fare classes on the routes below: Flights marketed and operated by Shenzhen Airlines: domestic routes within Mainland China, routes between Mainland China and Hong Kong, Macao or Taiwan Region.
