> **Source:** [Air New Zealand partner page (cathaypacific.com)](https://www.cathaypacific.com/cx/en_HK/our-partners/air-new-zealand.html) and the official Cathay Status Points & Asia Miles calculator (API-observed, 2026-07-14).

Air New Zealand is a Cathay airline partner but **not** a oneworld member: flights marketed by NZ earn **Asia Miles only** (rate % × actual miles flown) and **zero Status Points** when credited to Cathay (see [[earning/partner-model]]).

## Earning by booking class

| Cabin | Fare classes | Scope | Asia Miles | Status Points |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Business | C, D, J, Z | all | 125% | 0 |
| Premium Economy | A, E, O, U | all | 110% | 0 |
| Economy | Y, B, M | all | 100% | 0 |
| Economy | H, Q, T, V, W | all | 50% | 0 |

## Eligibility

Asia Miles can be earned only in eligible fare classes on: - Auckland – Hong Kong and Hong Kong – Auckland flights marketed and operated by Air New Zealand - Auckland – Hong Kong and Hong Kong – Auckland codeshare flights marketed by Air New Zealand and operated by Cathay Pacific - Christchurch – Hong Kong and Hong Kong – Christchurch codeshare flights marketed by Air New Zealand and operated by Cathay Pacific
