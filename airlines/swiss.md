> **Source:** [SWISS partner page (cathaypacific.com)](https://www.cathaypacific.com/cx/en_HK/our-partners/swiss-air.html) and the official Cathay Status Points & Asia Miles calculator (API-observed, 2026-07-14).

SWISS is a Cathay airline partner but **not** a oneworld member: flights marketed by LX earn **Asia Miles only** (rate % × actual miles flown) and **zero Status Points** when credited to Cathay (see [[earning/partner-model]]).

## Earning by booking class

| Cabin | Fare classes | Scope | Asia Miles | Status Points |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| First | A, F | all | 150% | 0 |
| Business | C, D, J, Z | all | 125% | 0 |
| Business | P | all | 100% | 0 |
| Premium Economy | E, G | all | 110% | 0 |
| Premium Economy | N | all | 100% | 0 |
| Economy | B, M, U, Y | all | 100% | 0 |
| Economy | H, Q, S, V, W | all | 50% | 0 |

## Eligibility

Asia Miles can be earned only in eligible classes on below routes: - Between Zurich and Berlin, Brussels, Florence, Geneva, Hamburg, Hong Kong, Stuttgart, Venice on flight marketed and operated by SWISS. - Between Vienna and Düsseldorf, Frankfurt, Zurich on codeshare flights marketed by SWISS and operated by Austrian Airlines. - Between Hong Kong and Auckland, Cairns, Melbourne, Sydney, Zurich on codeshare flights marketed by SWISS and operated by Cathay Pacific. - Between Frankfurt and Berlin, Brussels, Budapest, Dresden, Hamburg, Hannover, Munich, Nuremberg, Oslo, Stuttgart on codeshare flights marketed by SWISS and operated by Lufthansa.
