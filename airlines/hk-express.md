> **Source:** [Can I earn Asia Miles for HK Express flight bookings? (Cathay FAQ)](https://www.cathaypacific.com/cx/en_HK/faqs/cathay-membership-programme/asia-miles/redeeming-hk-express-flights/can-i-earn-asia-miles-for-hk-express-flight-bookings.html)

HK Express is Cathay Group's low-cost carrier, but UO flight bookings **do not earn Asia
Miles or Status Points**. It is a redemption partner only:

- Asia Miles can be **redeemed** for HK Express tickets ("Full Miles for Flight Ticket",
  including Essential fare category).
- The only indirect earning route is credit-card spend (e.g. the Standard Chartered Cathay
  Mastercard earns miles on eligible HK Express spending — HK market).
- UO is not in the Cathay Status Points & Asia Miles calculator's supported airline list.
