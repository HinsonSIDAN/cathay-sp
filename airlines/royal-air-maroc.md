Royal Air Maroc is a oneworld member: flights marketed by AT earn **Asia Miles** (rate % × actual miles flown) **and Status Points** (zone lookup by SP tier) when credited to Cathay. Zones and the SP tier matrix are defined in [[earning/partner-model]].

## Eligibility

Asia Miles can be earned only in eligible fare classes on: - Flights marketed and operated by Royal Air Maroc - Flight marketed by Royal Air Maroc and operated by one world® airlines Please note that itineraries involving Jeddah (JED) and Medina (MED) are only eligible for accrual but not redemption.
