> **Source:** [Austrian Airlines partner page (cathaypacific.com)](https://www.cathaypacific.com/cx/en_HK/our-partners/austrian-airlines.html) and the official Cathay Status Points & Asia Miles calculator (API-observed, 2026-07-14).

Austrian Airlines is a Cathay airline partner but **not** a oneworld member: flights marketed by OS earn **Asia Miles only** (rate % × actual miles flown) and **zero Status Points** when credited to Cathay (see [[earning/partner-model]]).

## Earning by booking class

| Cabin | Fare classes | Scope | Asia Miles | Status Points |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Business | C, D, J, Z | all | 125% | 0 |
| Business | P | all | 100% | 0 |
| Economy | B, M, U, Y | all | 100% | 0 |
| Economy | H, Q, S, V, W | all | 50% | 0 |

## Eligibility

Asia Miles can be earned only in eligible fare classes on below routes: - Between Vienna and Düsseldorf, Frankfurt, Zurich on flights marketed and operated by Austrian Airlines; - Between Hong Kong and Auckland, Cairns, Melbourne, Sydney on codeshare flights marketed by Austrian Airlines and operated by Cathay Pacific; - Between Frankfurt and Berlin, Brussels, Budapest, Dresden, Hamburg, Hannover, Munich, Nuremberg, Oslo, Stuttgart on codeshare flights marketed by Austrian Airlines and operated by Lufthansa; - Between Zurich and Berlin, Brussels, Florence, Geneva, Hamburg, Stuttgart, Venice on codeshare flights marketed by Austrian Airlines and operated by SWISS.
