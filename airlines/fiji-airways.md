> **Source:** [Fiji Airways partner page (cathaypacific.com)](https://www.cathaypacific.com/cx/en_HK/our-partners/fiji-airways.html) and the official Cathay Status Points & Asia Miles calculator (API-observed, 2026-07-14).

Fiji Airways is a oneworld member: flights marketed by FJ earn **Asia Miles** (rate % × actual miles flown) **and Status Points** (zone lookup by SP tier) when credited to Cathay. Zones and the SP tier matrix are defined in [[earning/partner-model]].

## Eligibility

Asia Miles can be earned only in eligible fare classes on - Flights marketed and operated by Fiji Airways; - Flights marketed by Fiji Airways and operated by Cathay Pacific.
