Royal Jordanian is a oneworld member: flights marketed by RJ earn **Asia Miles** (rate % × actual miles flown) **and Status Points** (zone lookup by SP tier) when credited to Cathay. Zones and the SP tier matrix are defined in [[earning/partner-model]].

| Economy | Y | all | 100% | F | 5 | 10 | 20 | 25 | 35 | 40 |

| Economy | L, M, N, Q, S, V | all | 50% | H | 5 | 10 | 15 | 15 | 20 | 25 |

## Eligibility

Asia Miles can be earned only in eligible fare classes on flights marketed and operated by Royal Jordanian, as well as on codeshare flights operated by all one world® carriers.
