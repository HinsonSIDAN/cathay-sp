S7's oneworld membership has been **suspended since 2022** (Russia sanctions). The Cathay
partner page no longer exists and the calculator returns no records for S7 on any probed
route (including DME–LED and DME–OVB), although S7 still appears in the calculator page's
stale airline config list.

No Asia Miles or Status Points earning is available. See [[airlines]] for current partners.
