LATAM Airlines is a Cathay airline partner but **not** a oneworld member: flights marketed by LA earn **Asia Miles only** (rate % × actual miles flown) and **zero Status Points** when credited to Cathay (see [[earning/partner-model]]).

## Eligibility

- LATAM flights eligible for miles accrual and redemption: - Flights marketed and operated by LATAM. - Flights marketed by LATAM and operated by Cathay Pacific. - Flights marketed by LATAM and operated by the affiliates LATAM Airlines Argentina / LATAM Airlines Brazil / LATAM Airlines Colombia / LATAM Airlines Ecuador / LATAM Express / LATAM Airlines Peru. - The following list indicates the non-applicable domestic flights of each region for each fare class. - Fare class K: Argentina. - Fare class M, L and V: Argentina and Peru. - Fare class S: Argentina, Peru and Ecuador. - Fare class N, Q and O: Argentina, Chile and Peru and Ecuador. - Participating affiliates are: - LATAM Airlines Argentina. - LATAM Airlines Brazil. - LATAM Airlines Colombia. - LATAM Airlines Ecuador. - LATAM Express. - LATAM Airlines Peru.
