## Key facts at a glance

- Silver 300 SP · Gold 600 · Diamond 1,200 (+ at least one CX sector per membership year);
  Diamond Exec (2,400) arrives 1 Jan 2027.
- CX-marketed flights: Asia Miles = Status Points × 100, always.
- Status Points come **only** from Cathay Pacific and oneworld airlines; other partners
  earn Asia Miles only. HK Express earns nothing.
