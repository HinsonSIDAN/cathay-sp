Qatar Airways prices HKG⇄Europe business aggressively (indicative HK$18,000–23,000
round trip, 2026), and the mandatory Doha connection is a textbook
[[strategies/sector-splitting]] win under the partner matrix:

| Sector | Distance / partner zone | SP (business tier B) |
|---|---|---|
| HKG⇄DOH | 3,966mi → Medium-2 ([[routes/doh-hkg]]) | 60 × 2 = 120 |
| DOH⇄LHR (or most of Europe) | 3,257mi → Medium-1 ([[routes/doh-lhr]]) | 45 × 2 = 90 |
| **Round trip total** | | **210 SP** |

Verified against [[airlines/qatar-airways]] (J/C/D/I/R = tier B). For comparison, a
CX HKG⇄LHR nonstop earns 200 SP in Business Light at a typically higher fare — and on
QR you ride Qsuite.

## Variations

- **Shorter Europe legs** (DOH⇄IST/ATH ~Short) earn 25/sector instead of 45 — pick a
  Medium-1 destination (LHR, CDG, FRA, most of Western Europe) for the full 210.
- **Longer QR sectors**: DOH⇄JFK is Long (75 SP), DOH⇄SYD/AKL are Ultra-long (85 SP) —
  see the 155-sector registry [[airlines/qatar-airways-routes]] for every option with
  resolved SP.
- **Ex-Europe origins**: QR's famous cheap ex-Scandinavia/ex-Balkans business fares
  (to Asia via DOH) earn the same split SP at even lower prices when you can position.
- Economy versions work too but the tier drops steeply (Y = tier F: 25+20 per direction).

## Caveats

- QR P class (discounted business) earns only 75%/tier G — check the booking class
  before buying ([[airlines/qatar-airways]]).
- Doha transit adds hours vs a nonstop; time-value is the real cost.
- No SP counts toward status without at least one CX sector in the membership year.
