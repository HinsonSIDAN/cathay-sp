Cathay prices the *same aircraft, same cabin* very differently depending on where the
ticket originates. Ex-Hong Kong long-haul business is premium-priced; ex-mainland-China,
ex-Taipei and ex-Southeast-Asia returns **via HKG** are sold far cheaper ‚Äî and because
they add connecting sectors, they earn **more** Status Points, not fewer
(see [[strategies/sector-splitting]] for the math).

## Ex-Mainland (PEK/PVG) ‚Äî the strongest version

Instead of HKG‚áÑSFO nonstop, buy PEK‚áÑSFO (or PVG‚áÑSFO) via HKG as one ticket:
PEK ‚úà HKG ‚úà SFO ‚úà HKG ‚úà PEK, all in **Business Light** (D/P/I ‚Üí 35 SP Short-T1,
100 SP Long per sector ‚Äî verified in [[airlines/cathay-pacific]]):

| Sector | CX zone | SP each way | Round trip |
|---|---|---|---|
| PEK‚áÑHKG | Short ‚Äì Type 1 | 35 | 70 |
| HKG‚áÑSFO | Long | 100 | 200 |
| **Total** | | | **270 SP** |

Compare the alternatives (verified against [[routes/hkg-sfo]]):

| Option | SP | Indicative cost (2026) |
|---|---|---|
| PEK‚áÑSFO via HKG, Business Light | **270** | RMB 18,000‚Äì24,000 (~HK$20,000‚Äì26,000) |
| HKG‚áÑSFO nonstop, Business Light | 200 | HK$45,000+ |
| HKG‚áÑSFO nonstop, Business Flex | 260 | HK$65,000‚Äì80,000 |

More points than a nonstop **Flex**, at roughly a third of the Light price. You need a
cheap one-way **positioning flight** (or miles redemption) to Beijing/Shanghai to open the
itinerary; HKG works as a transit-without-entry point mid-ticket.

## Ex-Taipei / Ex-Southeast Asia (TPE, KUL, BKK‚Ä¶)

Same mechanics, smaller feeder: TPE ‚úà HKG ‚úà SFO ‚úà HKG ‚úà TPE in Business Light:

| Sector | CX zone | SP each way | Round trip |
|---|---|---|---|
| TPE‚áÑHKG | Ultra-short | 25 | 50 |
| HKG‚áÑSFO | Long | 100 | 200 |
| **Total** | | | **250 SP** |

Indicative cost HK$22,000‚Äì27,000 (2026 community-reported). Taipei doubles as a cheap,
pleasant "opening city" for repeated runs; KUL adds a Short-T1 feeder (35 SP each way)
instead.

## Caveats

- The saving is a **point-of-sale/origin pricing** effect and can vanish in fare updates;
  always compare live prices.
- The whole journey must stay on eligible revenue fare classes; missed positioning
  flights can cascade into the main ticket.
- Mainland-origin tickets are RMB-priced; transit rules in HKG are trivial
  (transit-without-entry), but PEK/PVG opening requires being there.
