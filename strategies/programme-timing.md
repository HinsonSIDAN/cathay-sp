When you earn matters almost as much as how cheaply. The 2027 relaunch
([[programme/changes-2027]]) creates one-off and permanent timing effects:

## 2026 is the decisive year

**Status for 2027 is determined by SP earned 1 Jan – 31 Dec 2026** against the new
thresholds. Points earned in 2025 don't carry into that assessment — a member who was
comfortably Gold on 2025 flying but flies little in 2026 gets downgraded for 2027.
Concentrate planned runs (e.g. [[strategies/ex-origin-fares]]) inside calendar 2026.

## From 2027: overshooting is no longer wasted

- **No reset on upgrade** — 800 SP in one year takes you through Silver *and* Gold.
  Pre-2027, points reset at each upgrade, so big runs early in a membership year could
  "waste" points; from 2027 a single mega-run through multiple tiers is efficient.
- **Rollover (Gold and above)** — excess SP carry over, up to 50% of the highest
  achieved status's requirement (Gold 300 / Diamond 600 / Diamond Exec 1,200). Landing at
  ~900 SP as a Gold means starting the next year 300 SP ahead: aim runs at
  threshold + rollover cap, not just the threshold.
- **Diamond reserve** — every 6,000 lifetime SP since 2016 banks a complimentary
  Diamond year once you've held Diamond for 5 years. Long-horizon runners should track
  lifetime totals, not just annual.

## Use mid-status benefits before they end

Until 31 Dec 2026, current-programme mid-status benefits still trigger — Gold members
earn 4 single-sector Bookable Upgrades at 1,000 SP, Diamond at 1,600 SP. These
disappear in 2027 (mid-status benefits earned in 2026 stay valid 12 months). If a run
lands you near 1,000/1,600 in 2026, the upgrade vouchers are real extra value.

## Credit-card top-up

The Standard Chartered Cathay Mastercard adds up to 100 SP/year at
HK$100,000 spend = 20 SP — see [[programme/credit-card-earning]] for the block/cap
rules and the critical posting-lag detail (Q4 spend posts the following year).
Best used to bridge a <100 SP shortfall, planned by September.

## Perennial rules

- **One eligible CX sector per membership year** is required for any status — an
  all-partner or all-card year earns SP but no tier.
- SP post after flight completion; year-end runs should land with buffer days, and
  partner postings can take longer than CX.
- Earning tables can change with notice (last: Aug 2025) — recheck
  [[airlines/cathay-pacific]] before committing a long campaign.
