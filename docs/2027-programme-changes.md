## Status thresholds from 2027

| Status | Status Points required (calendar year) |
|--------|-----|
| Green | 0 (joining tier) |
| Silver | 300 |
| Gold | 600 |
| Diamond | 1,200 |
| **Diamond Exec** (new) | 2,400 |
| Diamond Plus | Invitation only — based on annual fare spending on Cathay Pacific flights |

## Key changes vs the pre-2027 programme

1. **No Status Points reset on upgrade.** SP no longer reset to zero when you upgrade;
   multiple status upgrades are possible within one calendar year (e.g. 800 SP earned in
   2027 → Silver at 300, then Gold at 600; that Gold is valid until 31 January 2029).
2. **Standardised membership cycle.** SP are earned per calendar year (1 Jan – 31 Dec);
   points earned in a year determine the following year's status. A higher status
   unlocked mid-year starts the next day and lasts until the end of the following year.
3. **Status Points rollover (Gold and above).** Excess SP roll over to the next year, up
   to 50% of the requirement of the highest status achieved that year:
   - Gold: up to 300 SP
   - Diamond: up to 600 SP
   - Diamond Exec: up to 1,200 SP
4. **New tier: Cathay Diamond Exec** (2,400 SP), positioned between Diamond and the
   invitation-only Diamond Plus. Dedicated Relationship Managers and elevated service.
5. **Diamond membership reserve.** Members who have held Diamond status for 5+ years
   (non-consecutive counts) receive one complimentary Diamond membership (up to one year)
   for every 6,000 SP earned since 2016. No expiry; non-transferable.
6. **Mid-status benefits discontinued** from 1 Jan 2027 (mid-status benefits earned in
   2026 stay valid 12 months from issue). Upgrade Passes (formerly Bookable Upgrades) and
   complimentary Gold companion membership become status benefits instead.

## 2026 transition arrangements

- Memberships in force keep their current status/benefits until their current expiry
  date (no extensions).
- A renewal, upgrade or downgrade during 2026 (under existing rules) yields a status
  valid until 31 December 2026; the associated SP balance resets under the old rules.
- **Status for 2027 is determined by SP earned 1 Jan – 31 Dec 2026** measured against the
  *new* thresholds. Cathay provides a personalised "2027 Status Tracker" during 2026.

All dates/times in Hong Kong Time.
