## Kind tags

- **reference** — lookup material (earning tables, thresholds, zone definitions)
- **spec** — a model/mechanic explained as source of truth (e.g. the partner SP matrix)
- **guide** — how-to / worked walkthroughs
- **index** — folder index entries
- **policy** — rules for this KB itself
- **methodology** — how the data was gathered and verified
- **meta** — about the KB, not about Cathay
- **glossary** — term/vocabulary definitions

## Domain tags

- **earning** — how flights turn into SP/miles
- **status-points** — SP-specific mechanics and thresholds
- **programme** — membership programme rules (tiers, cycles, 2027 changes)
- **airline** — a specific airline's earning entry
- **zones** — distance zone definitions
- **oneworld** — oneworld-member partner (earns SP)
- **partner** — non-alliance partner (Asia Miles only)
