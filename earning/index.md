How a flight turns into Status Points and Asia Miles. Two distinct models, both table
lookups on distance zone + booking class:

1. **CX-marketed flights** — [[airlines/cathay-pacific]]: SP by zone × cabin × fare type,
   Asia Miles = SP × 100.
2. **Partner-marketed flights** — [[earning/partner-model]]: Asia Miles = rate% × miles
   flown; Status Points from the universal tier × zone matrix (oneworld members only).
