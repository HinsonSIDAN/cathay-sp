Zones for **CX-marketed flights**, effective 20 Aug 2025, based on miles flown between
origin and destination. (Partner-marketed flights use the legacy zones — see
[[earning/partner-model]].)

| Zone | Miles | Examples from/to Hong Kong |
|---|---|---|
| Ultra-short | 1 – 750 | Hanoi, Manila, Taipei |
| Short – Type 1 | 751 – 2,750 | Beijing, Seoul, Singapore |
| Short – Type 2 | 751 – 2,750 (to/from Japan, Indonesia, Sri Lanka, Nepal, Bangladesh, India) | Jakarta, Mumbai, Tokyo |
| Medium | 2,751 – 5,000 | Dubai, Melbourne, Sydney |
| Long | 5,001 – 7,500 | London, Paris, San Francisco |
| Ultra-long | 7,501+ | Chicago, New York, Toronto |

The 20 Aug 2025 change split Short into Type 1/Type 2 (previously one Short zone) and
merged 2,751–5,000 miles into a single Medium zone (previously two).
